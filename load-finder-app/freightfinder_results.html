FreightFinder.com: The ONLY 100% Free Load Board

Try our free load search using the search bar below. It is simple, offers real-time visibility, and does not require any login or even an account. Narrow down the search criteria using the options listed, namely whether you are searching for freight loads or available trucks, equipment type, and either origin / destination cities in North America if you are using radius search, or origin and destination state if you are using the state to state search. Once you are ready, click the “go” button.

The results will display available loads or trucks matching your search criteria. You can have more results listed by removing criteria, or expanding the radius to a wider range.

Most loads listed are full truck loads for vans or flatbeds. Clicking on the date field on a given load or truck, will display pertinent load detail information regarding the given load such as rate, stops, and other important comments. This allows truck drivers to find a load matching their needs with greater accuracy.

Clicking on the company name from the search results page will display all the given company’s available loads or trucks.

Free mileage and routing information is included as well. Simply search for a load or truck, click on the company displaying the load. When you find a load on our loadboard and click into the details page, you will now see a link at the bottom of the load details screen reading "Mileage and routing, click here". Clicking this link will open a Google map prefilled with the mileage and general driving directions from the origin to destination of the given load.
